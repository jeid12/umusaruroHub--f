ABOUT COMPANY

Our main focus is on Sustainable Development Goal number 2. To achieve Zero Hunger for every household. Building a sustainable agriculture and thereafter food security requires all sectors in both government and Non governmental. Myshamba Express has the desire to be part of this journey, to work with farmers from the grass roots by Equipping them with the necessary knowledge on Agri-Business through Training & Development, Give accessibility to Farm Inputs, Link farmers to Financing and offer them a platform to Market their farm produce.

OUR GOALS

  • To train 100,000+ farmers annually both physically and virtually in various areas of agriculture such as dairy farming, Poultry, fisheries and so on
  • To offer farmers a seamless process of ordering and delivering farm inputs to their farms within 24 hours
  • Improve accessibility to financing by farmer through loans and grants
  • Linking over 50,000 farmers to the market by giving them a platform to sell their farm produce in 2023

Our Vision

To become the Farmers' One-stop online Shop

At umusaruroHub, our vision is clear: to become the Farmers' One-stop Online Shop. We are dedicated to revolutionizing the way farmers access essential resources, creating a seamless and comprehensive platform that meets all your agricultural needs.

Our Mission

Support sustainable agriculture

Build a sustainable Agriculture through Training & development, accessible farm inputs, financing and linking farmers to Markets.
